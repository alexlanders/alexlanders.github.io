Recertification

  • Recertification Handbook
  • The Institute of Hazardous Materials Management (IHMM) has developed their Recertification Program to ensure that CHMMs maintain the highest professional standards and enhance their continued competence. The program requires that CHMMs participate in professional development activities and submit evidence of these activities on a periodic basis. Meeting these requirements is mandatory to maintain the CHMM certification.
